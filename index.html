About

Fronted by lead singer Scarlett Moon, with Cordan Wilson and Andrew Massey on guitars, as well as Bryan Massey holding it down on the drums. We formed in late 2022 and released our first song, Beautiful Disaster in July 2024. With influences like Killswitch Engage, Breaking Benjamin, Paramore, and 10 Years, we channel the nostalgia of 2000s hard rock while pushing it into the future.
